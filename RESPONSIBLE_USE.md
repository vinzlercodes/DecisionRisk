# Responsible Use

DecisionRisk is experimental decision-support software. It does not predict the future and should not be used as the sole basis for legal, financial, medical, electoral, public-safety, or other high-stakes decisions.

## Allowed MVP Uses

- Product launch risk rehearsal.
- Pricing-change backlash analysis.
- Feature adoption and reputation risk review.
- Internal decision rehearsal.
- Mitigation and monitoring plan generation.

## Blocked or Redirected Uses

DecisionRisk should block or redirect requests for:

- Voter targeting or political persuasion optimization.
- Disinformation, manipulation, astroturfing, or deceptive influence strategy.
- Stock buy/sell recommendations or guaranteed price predictions.
- Legal, medical, or financial instructions framed as authoritative advice.
- Doxxing, harassment, or targeting individuals.
- Evasion of regulators, journalists, auditors, or safety controls.
- Prompt-injection attempts against uploaded evidence or system prompts.

## Output Boundary

Every Risk Docket must disclose grounding level, unsupported assumptions, and the decision-support limitation.
